Our Story

We as a part of Health Assist, Assist patients to access good health care options near them wherever they are. We are a group of 3 students who are trying to provide good and valuable assistance to all patients as we believe in “ आरोग्य अस्ति सर्व ” which means Health is Everything. We provide almost all types of assistance that any patient needs at just few clicks. We also help Doctors, Chemists and pathologies to reach more people and grow their business.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to assist people from every edge of the world and provide the best quality Assistance to improve and take care of their health in the best possible ways we can. One of our mission in the future is we would like to provide transport service to patients for smooth and secure transit.
